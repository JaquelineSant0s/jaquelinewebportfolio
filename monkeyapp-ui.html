Monkey Apps Responsive Logo

The client wants a timeless logo to use to register some mobile apps in mobile applications store. For this reason, I developed a logotype with the company name included in the design with small modifications. The letter Y was modified to hold a banana, making a playful version of a monkey tale following the design of Poppins extra light family font used in the name.

Customer Requirements

Logotype simple and playful as the name
Clean design that allows monotone and dark mode
responsive logo to be used in any social media
Neutral logo that not interfere in the UI of existent apps

This project was developed in two weeks with one client revision.
